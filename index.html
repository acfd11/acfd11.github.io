2nd International Retreat on Vorticity and Vortex Dynamics
The first International Retreat on Vorticity Aerodynamics was sucessfully held in Beijing, 2009, as an outgrowth of four previous retreats on vorticity and vortex dynamics held in Beijing for Chinese scientists and graduate students. The 2nd international retreat, now named International Retreat on Vorticity and Vortex Dynamics (IRVD), will be held in August of 2013 in China. The Retreat will bring together, from various points of the world, scholars and teachers of vorticity dynamics for intensive discussions of this subject, with focal applications in aerodynamics and flow control.

The 2013 IRVD will provide the international community in vorticity and vortex dynamics a forum for dialogue and personal interaction. Speakers at the 2013 Retreat are encouraged to discuss, in addition to research accomplishments, topics normally excluded from formal technical presentations: open issues and challenges deserving further research effort, promising applications of vorticity and vortex dynamics, pitfalls to avoid, etc.

The Retreat will feature keynote lectures, regular lectures, and poster presentations. To facilitate the sharing of ideas, formal presentations will be allocated approximately one half of the time of each technical session. The remaining time will be for audience participation: questions, answers, and brief remarks from the audience. In additional to technical sessions, evening programs and post-retreat excursions will provide opportunities for exchange of thoughts in less formal surroundings.

The Organizing Committee of the 2013 Retreat believes that the best avenue into the understanding of the flight of aerial animals and the swimming of aquatic animals is through vorticity and vortex dynamics, and such understanding will lead to improvements in the design of future aircraft and spacecraft. It hopes that the Retreat will enhance the research interest and experience of the participants. The Committee invites you to participate in the Retreat next year and, before that, to join us in the planning of the Retreat.

Symposium Topics:
The focus of the Retreat will be on the advances in theories, experiments, numerical simulations, and practical applications of vortex dynamics and vorticity aerodynamics. New insight into physical understanding and works that combine studies at both fundamental and applied levels, and/or combine theoretical/numerical and experimental studies are most welcome. Depending on the submitted contributions, main topics will be selected from, but not limited to:

* Vorticity aerodynamics theory and applications;
* Fundamental problems of vortex dynamics and vorticity aerodynamics in incompressible and compressible flows;
* Vorticity and vortices in air and water vehicle, bird flight, fish swimming, and other biolocomotive motion;
* Vortex instability and breakdown;
* Vortical structures in wall-bounded and free turbulent shear layers;
* Vortex sound and vertical flow-induced vibration;
* Active and passive control of vortical flows and turbulence;
* Vortex dynamics in geophysical flows, civil engineering, energy resources, and biomedical science, etc.
